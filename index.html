"what do you know about Glagah?"

Glagah is a type of wild plant that can grow and reproduce quickly. Glagah belongs to the Poaceae family such as bamboo, rice, wheat, corn and barley. This plant has a segmented stem and can grow from 1 to 3 meters. It has strong and deep roots. This plant can grow in various habitats such as forests, gardens, rivers, lakes, swamps and rice fields. Usually Glagah is often considered a pest because it grows wild and can interfere with other plants, therefore, farmers usually cut and throw this plant away. However, did you know that this plant has its own benefits? Among the benefits are as follows:
  • Prevents soil erosion because it has deep roots, rhizomes and fast reproduction, making this plant suitable for erosion prevention.
  • Serves to absorb water mixed with organic waste such as animal and human waste because it is able to absorb water in large quantities.
  • Can be used as animal feed.
  • And useful as a raw material for making grass brooms.
In this case, CV.IndoRayung Nusantara together with the Cikadu Villager utilize Glagah plants as raw materials for our processed products, one of which is a grass broom. The raw materials we deliver are sorted with the best quality. We are ready to be the best manufacturer and supplier of grass brooms or materials with high quality products in large quantities for all your needs.
